In Loving Memory

Laura Grace Stubbs

September 17, 1953 — April 14, 2026

Scroll to remember

Laura Grace Stubbs, a devoted servant of Jehovah, fell asleep in death on April 14, 2026, at age 72.

Born September 17, 1953, she was known for her strong faith, kindness, and dedication to her ministry as one of Jehovah's Witnesses.

She was preceded in death by her son, Matthew. She is lovingly remembered by his wife, Ronda; her daughters, Tiffany Smith (Michael Smith) and Elizabeth Brandimore (Patrick Brandimore); and her grandchildren, Oliver, Eliott, Savannah, Zaden, and Addison.

Laura will be deeply missed, and her legacy of faith and love will endure.
